Why we built this

In Tamil Nadu, millions use both Siddha/Ayurvedic medicines and modern allopathic drugs together — without knowing the risks. Dangerous combinations like Warfarin + Turmeric or BP medicine + Sarpagandha can be life-threatening. Sevvi makes it easy for everyone to check before mixing.

Who it's for

Patients managing multiple medicines, elderly people on long-term medication, caregivers, and anyone who takes both traditional herbs and modern medicines. No medical knowledge needed — just type or speak.

How it works

Sevvi uses a curated database of known drug-herb interactions specific to Tamil Nadu context. For combinations not in the database, Claude AI analyzes and explains in both Tamil and English.
